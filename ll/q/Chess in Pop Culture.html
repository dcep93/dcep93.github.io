Smiths' Blurb

Click to view blurb
Click to hide blurb
“In chess, we get a fighting game which is purely intellectual and excludes chance.” - Richard Reti, chess player and author

Perhaps this is what draws authors, songwriters, painters, and just about everyone else to use chess play and pieces in their creations. Whether its a metaphor for life, an easily recognized design component, or just to show that a character is intelligent, using a chessboard or piece is an easy way to get your target audience to easily understand a concept - even those who have never played the game.

It was hard for us to pick out just 12 questions about chess as it appears in pop culture but thanks to our very intelligent testers, we did it.

We hope you enjoy the quiz!
