Smith's Blurb

Click to view blurb
Click to hide blurb
The album cover is to some (myself included) not merely a necessary part of selling music, or worse, an unnecessary frill, but is an essential and integral part of the artistic endeavour itself, an extension of the soul of the music (when it’s done right). The world of album cover art is incredibly rich and varied, with no shortage of quirks and oddities, and I’ve attempted to capture that in this quiz. Will it chronicle the timeless imagery that has packaged some of the most beloved music in history, or explore the arcane and bemusing work of unsung masters of the practice? Well, probably both, and more.

I was delighted to be given the opportunity to craft a second instalment of this subject, a subject which represents the intersection of my obsession and my vocation (music and graphic design). As with the first time around, the goal is to strive for a PG on the ModKos scale in order to capture both the music lover and a wider audience, with trivia extending beyond mere identification of music and musicians. Just Images Album Cover Art 2 will cover several eras and genres, though with less of a focus on the history of the medium than its predecessor had.
