Smith's Blurb

Welcome to Clues in the Key of C, a general knowledge quiz with a twist: part of each clue is an audio file with a series of notes. You'll need to translate that series of notes in one of three ways:
(1) into the letters corresponding to the notes (C, D, E, etc.),
(2) into the numbers corresponding to the notes on a C major scale (C = 1, D = 2, E = 3, etc.), or
(3) into the solfège syllables for the notes on a C major scale (C = do, D = re, E = mi, etc.).
You won't be told which one to use for each question; you'll have to figure it out. I'd put this at a ModKos PG-13.

Let's run through an example. Say the audio file contained the note “E” twice.

(1) If the question were, "What is the last name of the modernist poet with these first two initials?”, the answer would be "cummings" for "e e cummings.”

(2) If the question were, instead, "What name is on the retired Los Angeles Lakers jersey with this number?", the answer would be "Abdul-Jabbar," since Kareem Abdul-Jabbar wore number 33.

(3) If the question were, instead, "What famous opera features a character with this first name?", the answer would be "La bòheme," which has a character named "Mimi."

While a good musical ear may help, I encourage tin-eared LLamas to give it a go: you can very likely figure out the notes if you try. (But remember: LearnedLeague rules prohibit "using musical instruments" to answer questions!) Deaf LLamas may be able to puzzle out some answers, but it will be a tough challenge.

To start you off on the right foot, the note in the first question is a C.

How to play an LL One-Day

• Answer the 12 questions.

• Designate 5 questions as Money Questions (by ticking the box next to the question).

• For each question answered correctly, you receive 15 points.

• For each correct Money Question, in addition to the 15, you receive the number of points equal to the percentage of participants who answer the question incorrectly (e.g. if 74% of the players get it wrong, you get 74 money points in addition to the 15).

• You must answer the Money Question correctly to receive the money points.

• The player who earns the most total points wins the LL One-Day.
