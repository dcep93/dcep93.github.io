Smith's Blurb

Click to view blurb
Click to hide blurb
This is, as you can probably gather from the title, a 1DS about the prolific German composer Richard Strauss (1864-1949). The quiz will try to cover a variety of the genres that Strauss contributed to over the course of his career and the differing compositional styles he employed, as well as some questions about musicians associated with his music in some significant way. Although a few questions have clues from general classical music knowledge or from other categories entirely, some knowledge of Strauss's music is required for most questions. Almost all questions have an attached audio clip that is not necessary for answering the question, so if you're interested in getting a small taste of his music this could be a good way to do so (even if the quality of my audio editing skills does leave something to be desired). Hope you enjoy!
