October 17, 2024  •  LL One-Day Special: United States/Mexico Relations

Smith's Blurb

Hello LLamas! I hope you enjoy this quiz on relations between the United States and Mexico. I want to emphasize up front that this is not specifically a quiz on political/historical foreign relations between the two countries, although some questions touch on that subject. Rather, it is a quiz on all types of cultural links between the two countries, including music, language, sports, food, and more. Some questions reflect very real connections between the culture and history of both countries, but other questions just reflect fun, quiz-appropriate coincidences I have found in my nearly two years living in Mexico City.

In the 12 questions, I tried to include most of the most important categories in a regular LL season, so you can expect at least one question on the bigger categories to appear. I would say this quiz will tilt towards below-average difficulty for those with a strong knowledge of Mexican history and culture (whether they are from the country itself, have roots, or just a strong interest in it) and be above-average difficulty for Americans with limited knowledge of Mexico.

1. If you remove a letter from the name of the second largest Metropolitan Area in Mexico by population (which contains the 9th largest city of the same name), you will get the name of this much smaller city in California, the setting of John Steinbeck's novel "Cannery Row". Name either the metropolitan area or the city.
monterey
MONTEREY / MONTERREY
2. Something I didn't know before I came to Mexico: A "[BLANK] Kid" is a perjorative term for a person of Mexican descent (or any Latin-American country) who grew up in the United States but doesn't speak Spanish. The term is based on an incorrect conjugation of an important verb in Spanish. What two words, reflecting that incorrect conjugation, fill in the blank?
no hablas
NO SABO
3. Henry Lane Wilson was an American government official notorious for his involvement in the 1913 coup in Mexico that removed Mexican President Francisco Madero. At the time, what position in the US government did Wilson, who served in government from the McKinley to Woodrow Wilson administrations, hold?
secretary state
UNITED STATES AMBASSADOR TO MEXICO
4. What is the stage name of Hassan Emilio Kabande Laija, whose collaboration "Ella Baila Sola" was the 5th most streamed spotify song in 2023? That stage name is the Spanish equivalent of a boxing term — add the modifier "Super" to that boxing term and you'll have Oscar De La Hoya's weight class when he won his first world title.
<blank>
PESO PLUMA
5. Take tacos al pastor (marinated pork and pineapple), put them in a flour tortilla, and grill with cheese like a quesadilla. What dish have you made? The name of the dish is thought to come from American diners preferring white flour tortillas. Make sure to use the right gender!
zapata
GRINGA
$
6. Name any one of the four Mexicans have been nominated for, or won, the Oscar for Best Cinematography since the year 2000. The list includes one man with an astonishing six nominations (several garnered while working with director Alejandro Iñárritu) and three wins in that time period, and one man who is more known in the film word for another, bigger role he plays on film sets.
cuaron
EMMANUEL LUBEZKI / RODRIGO PRIETO / ALFONSO CUARÓN / GUILLERMO NAVARRO

7. In Mexico, the name for hibiscus comes from the Caribbean island in which the plant was originally sourced, about 600 miles off the Yucatan peninsula. The island's name is shared with the neighborhood in which a 21st century U.S. president was born. Name the island/neighborhood.
jamaica
JAMAICA
8. Six Mexican states border the United States. One is Chihuahua, the biggest state by area in Mexico. Another is Baja California, whose El Chaparral Crossing is one of the busiest land border crossings in the world. Name any one of the other four.
norteno
COAHUILA / NUEVO LEÓN / SONORA / TAMAULIPAS
9. In 2018, Los Tigres del Norte, a band founded in Sinaloa, Mexico was given permission by a California government agency to perform in what unorthodox venue? The performance commemorated the 50th anniversary of another famous recording in the same venue, the first of a series of recordings made in similar locations.
cave
FOLSOM PRISON
10. What three words fill in the blank from this abbreviated quote from the United States' 1992 Presidential Debate: "We have got to stop sending jobs overseas...[It's] pretty simple: If you're paying $12, $13, $14 an hour for factory workers and you can move your factory south of the border, pay a dollar an hour for labor... and you don't care about anything but making money, there will be a [BLANK] going south."
<blank>
GIANT SUCKING SOUND
$
11. A Major League Soccer (MLS) franchise that existed from 2005-14 was a subsidiary of the second winningnest (by league titles) club in Liga MX history. That Mexican club is often referred to by its nickname, which comes from the Spanish word for goats, and the American club took that nickname as its primary name. Provide the nickname.
cabraxy
CHIVAS (USA)

12. The Pancho Villa Expedition of 1916-17 was an operation carried out by the United States to capture the eponymous revolutionary after an attack he made across the border in New Mexico. The expedition was led by one of the most famous generals of World War I, and represented the first combat action of his aide, later one of the most famous generals of World War II. Name either general.
patton
JOHN J. PERSHING / GEORGE S. PATTON

How to play an LL One-Day

• Answer the 12 questions.

• Designate 5 questions as Money Questions (by ticking the box next to the question).

• For each question answered correctly, you receive 15 points.

• For each correct Money Question, in addition to the 15, you receive the number of points equal to the percentage of participants who answer the question incorrectly (e.g. if 74% of the players get it wrong, you get 74 money points in addition to the 15).

• You must answer the Money Question correctly to receive the money points.

• The player who earns the most total points wins the LL One-Day.
