Smith's Blurb

Click to view blurb
Click to hide blurb
Ah, cellists...easily the coolest members of the string section, if not the entire orchestra. There will be a fairly wide variety of categories within the topic, so you don't have to even personally know a cellist to play this 1DS, though of course that will help. I'd estimate the ModKos to be about PG-13. So rosin up your bow, walk out onto the stage, and play your heart out. And be sure to have a good chair for this. As a great cellist once said, "90% of cello playing is finding the right chair."
