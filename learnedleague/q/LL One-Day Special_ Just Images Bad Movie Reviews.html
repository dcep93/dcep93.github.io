Smith's Blurb

Welcome to Just Images: Bad Movie Reviews 1DS!

Ever come across a movie review where it's hilarious, unhinged or the reviewer completely missed the point / expected the movie to be something else?

Each question will be a screenshot of a movie review I pulled from the internet - predominantly a collection of Amazon or Letterboxd reviews over the years. I'm well aware that some of these reviews could apply to multiple movies and thus, in addition to the review itself, please note the year of release provided in the question text. Do note that some of the reviews provided also include the date on which the review was submitted, I would strongly recommend ignoring that information given that the date of review submission is not necessarily linked to when the movie was actually released.

The answer to each question is simply the name of the movie being reviewed.

Example Question

Year of Release - 1997
1 Star - The Boat Sinks

Answer - Titanic

In full transparency, I expect this to fall at a MODKOS G/PG level for the most part with relatively high scoring but why don't we all have a bit of fun in the midst of the typically super specialized off-season?

Best of luck!

How to play an LL One-Day

• Answer the 12 questions.

• Designate 5 questions as Money Questions (by ticking the box next to the question).

• For each question answered correctly, you receive 15 points.

• For each correct Money Question, in addition to the 15, you receive the number of points equal to the percentage of participants who answer the question incorrectly (e.g. if 74% of the players get it wrong, you get 74 money points in addition to the 15).

• You must answer the Money Question correctly to receive the money points.

• The player who earns the most total points wins the LL One-Day.
