1.  From his youth, Kaladin has had a special relationship with the Wind. Beginning in his time in Amaram's army, there seemed to be a particularly playful windspren who frequently pranked him. He later discovers that Sylphrena is not a windspren but what other variety of spren?

Click here to reveal answer
HONORSPREN

2.  A particular variety of rockbud is a common food source throughout Roshar, and the basis of Hearthstone's economy. Mature polyps are filled with grains that can be ground into a flour, steamed, or eaten plain. Soulcasters can also produce this grain, though the texture of Soulcast grain varies with the skill of the Soulcaster who produces it. Name this plant.

Click here to reveal answer
LAVIS

3.  Throughout the series, a certain character is alternatively referred to as a Voidbringer, Whineyspren, Dummyspren, Lamespren, Cowardspren, Wisdomspren (self-appelation), and a very regal fork. In order, what is this character's name and what type of spren is he actually? (Both name and spren type required)

Click here to reveal answer
WYNDLE, CULTIVATIONSPREN

4.  What is the name of the Worldhopper scholar who retrieves Shallan's notebooks from the bottom of the ocean after the disappearance of the Wind's Pleasure? Drawings and annotations from this scholar are found throughout the Stormlight books.

Click here to reveal answer
NAZH

5.  What type of spren is bonded in a singer's gemheart to create warform? This spren usually flees before a highstorm, requiring the listeners to capture them in crystals using human methodology.

Click here to reveal answer
PAINSPREN

6.  During Lift's pursuit of Nale in Yeddaw, her awesomeness was primarily fueled by what holiday food?

Click here to reveal answer
PANCAKES

7.  Prior to the Night of Sorrows, there were three secret organizations working to either manipulate or profit from events on Roshar. The Sons of Honor sought the return of the Heralds, the Ghostbloods sought to transport investiture to other worlds, and the Diagram sought to unite as much of humanity as possible to minimize losses after Odium's inevitable victory. Name the leader for each of these organizations. (Three names required)

Click here to reveal answer
RESTARES, THAIKADUR, TARAVANGIAN

8.  While imprisoned by Raboniel, Navani discovers a repeatable method to synthesize anti-Light. When trying to describe the nature of this anti-Light, she later wrote: "I find the closest model to be that of [redacted][redacted] in sound. A [redacted] waveform is not itself an opposite, but indeed the exact same waveform played opposite the primary one." Name the scientific phenomenon described here. This real-world phenomenon is the mechanism used to produce noise-cancelling headphones.

Click here to reveal answer
DESTRUCTIVE INTERFERENCE

9.  Which Herald worked as part of the Diagram and later agreed to serve Odium? Some of her contributions include tracking Death Rattles, writing the essay that undermined Dalinar's credibility, and driving crystal spikes through the eyes of Moash.

Click here to reveal answer
BATTAR

10.  At least two Dawnshards have been present on Roshar throughout the first era of the Stormlight Archives. Name the Commands associated with these two Dawnshards.

Click here to reveal answer
CHANGE, EXIST

11.  In an effort to deprive Odium of millenia of planning before launching his invasion of the cosmere, Dalinar renounces all oaths and forfeits his place as Honor's Vessel. This renunciation results in Taravangian becoming what entity with the full power of two Shards?

Click here to reveal answer
RETRIBUTION

12.  Always the consummate Windrunner, Kaladin accepts the charge to become a Herald in order to protect the spren. He is dubbed the Herald of Wind, Herald of Kings, and Herald of Second Chances--filling the vacancy in the Oathpact created by the destruction of which Herald? His predecessor was the King of the Heralds and fittingly, also the Patron of Windrunners.

Click here to reveal answer
JEZRIEN

Smith's Blurb

Click to hide blurb –
*****SPOILER WARNING*****
This One-Day covers all Stormlight Archive books, and contains spoilers. If you read the first couple books but haven't gotten around to tackling Wind and Truth yet, this is NOT the quiz for you. (Go read the books and then come back and we can talk.) If you are considering reading the series some day but have not yet, please do not even look at the questions. HERE BE SPOILERS. You have been warned.

Welcome to the world of Roshar! This is a quiz covering the world building, characters, plot, etc. of Brandon Sanderson's Stormlight Archive series. All questions are gettable from reading the Stormlight books, with no outside knowledge of the Cosmere or other Sanderson works required. By the same token, there are also not many questions that are accessible without having read (or listened to) the books. The goal has been to create something that will be fun for casual fans of the series, while still having enough meat to satisfy those of you who listen to Shardcast during your commute.

Life before death.
Strength before weakness.
Journey before destination.
