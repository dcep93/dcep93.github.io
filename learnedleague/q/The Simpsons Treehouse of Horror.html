7.  Bart and Lisa become Stretch Dude and Clobber Girl when an exploding candy x-ray machine gives them superpowers. They must save Lucy Lawless from The Collector. Lawless ends up saving the day and then demonstrates what superpower of her own? Lisa states that Xena can't do this, but is reminded that she's not Xena; she's Lucy Lawless.

8.  "Pathetic humans! They're showing a Halloween episode… in NOVEMBER!". Several times over the years, the new episode has actually premiered after Halloween due to scheduling conflicts with Fox's coverage of what event? In Treehouse of Horror XVI, Kang and Kodos attempt to speed up this event using an accelerator-ray, but cause the participants to move so fast that it creates a vortex that ends up destroying the totality of existence.

Click here to reveal answer

9.  One of the recurring elements of the Treehouse of Horror series is the "scary names" for the cast and crew in the credits. Most people have a different scary name each year, but developer and executive producer Sam Simon had the same alliterative name for 20 years. What word completes Sam Simon's scary name up through Treehouse of Horror XXII?

Click here to reveal answer
SAYONARA

10.  It's the 1996 election and candidates Bill Clinton and Bob Dole have been kidnapped and replaced by Kang and Kodos. The imposter Clinton gives a bizarre speech at the debate - "We must move forward, not backward; upward not forward; and always _____ toward _____." Fill in the missing words. (Note: the first redaction is the same word repeated 3 times. You only need to provide it once. Two answers required.)

Click here to reveal answer

11.  A beloved children's classic is trampled upon when what character, brought to life by Milhouse's imagination and tears, goes on a rampage of revenge after witnessing (and tasting) atrocities against his kind? Note: please name the character as he's called in this segment and not the original character he's based on.

Click here to reveal answer

He is first seen eating strange candy and flavored wax on the school bus and later is one of the early victims of the Nightmare Cafeteria. Who is this character? You might even say we just ate him and he's in our stomachs right now! (first name acceptable and diacritic not required)

Smith's Blurb

Click to view blurb +
Click to hide blurb –
Hello, everyone. You know, Halloween is a very strange holiday. Personally, I don't understand it. Kids worshiping ghosts, pretending to be devils. Oooh, things on TV that are completely not appropriate for younger viewers. Things like the following 1DS. Nothing seems to bother my kids. But this 1DS, which I totally wash my hands of, is really scary. So if you have sensitive LLamas, maybe you should tuck them into bed early tonight instead of writing Thorsten angry letters tomorrow. Thanks for your attention.

This quiz is designed to test your Simpsons knowledge - specifically about the Treehouse of Horror Halloween special episodes. Therefore, many questions will be YEKIOYD. The questions also lean heavily toward the earlier seasons.

A quick note about scoring - If the question asks for the name of a Simpsons character, please use the name that they are commonly referred to within the show and can distinguish them from other characters with the same last name. For example, “Simpson” alone would not be acceptable if the answer is “Homer Simpson”, but “Homer” would be. There will be reminders within the questions where this is applicable and I have made it so there are no situations where this could be confusing. If the question asks for the name of a real life person, the regular LL scoring rules will be applied.
