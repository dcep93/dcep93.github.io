Smith's Blurb

Click to view blurb +
Click to hide blurb –
The worst part of a game night is suddenly discovering a stray piece from a game you forgot to put away. This 1DS will re-enact that as you work out what board game unique pieces belong to. This quiz isn't simply all questions about that though, maybe some pieces will be connected via the same designer, or share other interesting connections. ModKos Rated R, you can always jump in and give it a try. Who knows maybe you'll find the game you want to play next, only to forget that one stray piece during clean-up.

The Alt Text will only describe QUESTION RELEVANT aspects of the images. Playing the games these pieces and cards came from is not required knowledge (though could be a slight advantage).
